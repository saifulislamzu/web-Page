Learn Global Skill Development, Be Self-Employed | The Best
Outsourcing Freelancing Training Center in Bangladesh

CodersTrust Bangladesh (CT) is a multinational company specializing in a blended Learn and Earn platform for people to learn globally demanded skills to earn money online and find jobs or improve careers. Founded by Bangladeshi-American global business leader Aziz Ahmad and Danish army captain Ferdinand Kearwolf, with the dream of making learning accessible to everyone.

CodersTrust BD is the first and largest International Freelancer and outsourcing training center in Dhaka, providing on-campus and online professional courses in Bangladesh. Students, graduates, job-holders, housewives, retirees, anyone can learn Graphic Design, Responsive Web Design, Digital Marketing, Android App Development, Accounts Management System, WordPress Customization, Content Writing, facebook Marketing, Web Development and more. Currently, Coderstrust is offering more than 20+ high demand courses.

The company currently has global offices in Denmark, Bangladesh, USA, and Kosovo and it has managed projects in 11 countries (India, Malaysia, Bhutan, Kosovo, Bangladesh, Iraq, Poland, Palestine, Honduras, Kenya, and Jordan).

Our mission is to transform human resources, predominantly youth to skilled professionals to improve lives and change people’s working patterns by providing outsourcing training to make a huge impact on revenue from earning online. We aim to transform the obstacles towards proper education and youth unemployment into an “investable” opportunity to be self employed and earn money from home by contributing online jobs from several freelance websites like Upwork, Fiverr, Freelancer, PeoplePerHour and so on.

We bridge the gap between the global and local workforce as well as the skill gap between supply and demand. We provide affordable skill development IT courses. As assistance to make money online, we provide free career counseling support 24/7 to help in choosing the right courses and learn the right skills.

We believe in a borderless world with equal opportunities for everyone. We believe the freelance marketplace will make this happen. With CodersTrust — making education available with high prospects in earning from home in Bangladesh.
